About Kinich

Kinich is a playable character from Genshin Impact, known as a Saurian Hunter with the ancient name "Malipo." He is frequently seen with K'uhual Ajaw, the self-proclaimed "Almighty Dragonlord."

In this website, you will find information about his lore, GIFs, images, and videos related to him.
